PRODUCTS

Our Collection crosses all boundaries and assortments with new technologies and performances. Engineered nature/tech blends use formaldeyde free coatings to deliver poetic, futuristic and dynamic effects. Lighter fleece and translucent jersey webs use light reflection mixed with soft metallised surfaces, and sensory fabrics mix a duality of sensation, contrasting the crisp and dry with a noisome papery touch or a muffled soft and pliant feel. hermo responsive and UV sensitive techno hybrids maintain performance with their responsible heritage. Even more eco sustainable qualities are delivered in a full range of new hi-tech organics, with Cottons, Recycled Pe, Recycled Stretch and Tencel. All are revealed in a new warm and familiar toned palette, in reversible dual textures, fancy jerseys, fleece and ribs. t is a conscious utilitarian collection with an easy performance for the new athleisure category.

NATURAL

Our grain of sand for a better tomorrow. Using only sustainable and renewable materials, we bring you fabrics with a minimal environmental footprint and a good optimistic attitude. All the aspects of the production process were taking under consideration to minimize ecological impact.
We also believe that taking care of the earth starts by cherishing yourself. In our Pure Life line, you will also find fabrics that nurture your skin, such as Seacell fabrics. Regenerated cellulosics like Tencel, lyocell, Modal, Viscose made from managed woodpulp sources in a closed loop system with minimal waste.

Providing our clients with contemporary solutions, is at the heart of our business. Continuous research into new fashion trends, and a constant search for new materials, techniques and finishings, enable our R&D team to provide the latest developments in fashion.

TECH&SPORTS

Continuous innovation is the motto for this product range. Working in strong partnership with clients, fiber producers and researchers of finishing products has lead us to a position where we are proud to be the first to bring to the market the latest developments of the textile industry. The combination of high levels of performance, protection and comfort with great aesthetics results in our range of fabrics especially suitable for sports activities.

Çulcuoğlu Fabrics

We have been devoted to producing knitted fabrics for more than 20 years. We manufacture innovative, trendy and technical knitted fabrics. We are expert with circular and warp knitted fabrics and we have wide variety of knitting fabric collection.

We make fabrics for the fashion, sport, causal, medical, lifestyle and industrial industry. We love creating fabrics for any needs, whether the need is trendy and colorful jacquards or technical and functional 3D spacer fabrics. We offer a close collaboration with our customers, where they can rely on a personal dialogue, our trend knowledge and our many years of expertise. We are always honest, quality oriented and cooperative when it comes to designing and producing our customers’ requests. We take pride in always being updated on the newest trends, techniques and technology.

To secure our high quality we have partners throughout the world; partners such as yarn mills, dye houses, trend agencies and etc. These collaborations are based on a mutual understanding of integrity, loyalty and quality.

Our customers are our livelihood and we offer them a good relation, good conditions and high quality products. Our customers can expect to get a high quality product, continuous product quality, a standardized quality control and service, a wide selection of yarns, compositions and fabrics, delivery on time, a short process, good prices and a fair complaint process.

We also offer environmental friendly yarns as well as non-mulesed wool, and all of our fabrics are produced in Turkey. Our customers can also request traceable wool and a transparent production, as we care for the environment, animals and people involved.
